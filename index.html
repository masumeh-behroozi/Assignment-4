Cultural Cities of Afghanistan

A calm cultural journey through Afghan cities, exploring traditions, crafts, poetry, and daily life.

Kabul

The vibrant capital of Afghanistan, Kabul is rich in history, bustling markets, traditional arts, and a diverse cultural life. It is a vivid example of unity among the country’s different cultures.

Herat

Herat has been a center of Persian literature and poetry for centuries, producing many renowned poets.

Bamyan

Famous for its stunning landscapes and historical heritage, including the iconic Buddhas of Bamyan, Bamyan is also a place where different cultures coexist in harmony.

Mazar e Sharif

Mazar-i-Sharif (center of Balkh Province) celebrates the annual Nowruz festival, attracting thousands of visitors to the city.

City of the Day — Bamyan
